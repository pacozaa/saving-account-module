## Business Requirements

### 1. Online Registration

a. Only new CUSTOMER can register by input both email and password. Then they need to fill-in personal information including citizen id, Thai name, and English name and PIN 6 digits number.

### 2. Creating a New Account

a. Both CUSTOMER can go to the bank asking TELLER to create a new account. Teller can create a new account using his/her citizen id, Thai name, and English name with or without a deposit.

b. System generates unique "account number" with 7 numeric characters â€“ for example "7777777" that will be use as a reference for deposit/money transfer.

### 3. Money Deposit

a. Only TELLER can deposit money to an existing account number. The amount must be 1 THB or more.

### 4. Account Information

a. Only CUSTOMERS can login and see his/her account (if the teller created)

### 5. Money Transfer

a. Only CUSTOMER can transfer money from his/her own account to any other existing account after login to system with PIN confirmation.

### 6. Bank Statement

a. Only CUSTOMER can ask for his/her bank statement of a specific month after login to the system with PIN confirmation. The list of transaction must be displayed from past to present.
